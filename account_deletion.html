Account Deletion

Effective date: 2026-01-19

The Service Provider respects your right to manage your personal information. Users of Our Bucket List may delete their account at any time directly within the Application.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, follow these steps within the Application:

  • Open the Application
  • Go to Settings
  • Select Profile Settings
  • Tap Delete Account

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

What Data is Deleted

When you delete your account, the Service Provider will permanently remove all data associated with your account, including:

  • Your email address
  • Your username
  • All content or data you uploaded to the Application

After deletion, the Service Provider will not retain any personal data from your account.
